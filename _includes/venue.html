Aizuwakamatsu City Culture Center

14-52, Jotomachi, Aizuwakamatsu, Fukushima, Japan

The Aizuwakamatsu City Culture Center is a facility built for various cultural activities.The facility is built on the territory of Tsurugajo castle which is a symbol of Fukushima Pref.Tsuruga castle is a famous castle known as the stage of the Boshin war of 1868, It's a historical incident representing Japan.
